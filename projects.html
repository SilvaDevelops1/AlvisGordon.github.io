Todo list and productivity management system

This project is set to be developed before the Personal Finance Tracker. It aims to help users stay organized and productive with a range of features designed to improve task management and efficiency.

  • Planned Features:
  • Visual Graphs: Track your progress and productivity with detailed graphs.
  • Calendar Integration: Sync with Google, Outlook, and Apple calendars for seamless scheduling.
  • Break Tracker: Receive reminders to take a 30-minute break after spending a set amount of time on a project.
  • Personalized Work/Study Plans: Get customized plans based on your habits to optimize your workflow.
  • XP and Achievements: Earn XP for completing tasks, level up, and unlock achievements to make productivity more engaging and fun.
  • Progress Sharing: Share your achievements and progress online with friends and family.
  • Business and Parent Plans: Includes a business plan for organizational use and a parent plan to track and support children’s progress and learning needs.
  • Goals/Sub-Goals/Categories/Main Tasks: Organize your tasks into goals, sub-goals, categories, and main tasks for better structure.
  • Timed Goals: Set due dates for goals to help manage deadlines.
  • Goal-Specific Notes: Add notes specific to each goal, sub-goal, or task.
  • Shopping List: Keep track of shopping items for added convenience.
  • Additional Features: More useful features will be added based on user feedback and input.
Note: This project will start when I have a bit more free time and the necessary equipment to develop it. I’m excited to build a system that is both functional and engaging, and I look forward to incorporating your suggestions and feedback.

Travel booking system

This upcoming project site will be your personal trip planner, designed to streamline your travel arrangements and make planning a breeze. It will allow you to search for flights with estimated prices, book hotels, and discover fun activities in your destination. The system will include a comprehensive planner to organize everything from flights and hotels to budgeting—integrating with the personal finance tracker to help you save for your dream vacation. You’ll be able to create and download a holiday bucket list with all the relevant details and car rental options, and save your planned trips to revisit when flights are cheaper or when you’ve saved enough. The system will also link to your To-Do List and Productivity Management System, ensuring you stay on top of your work and school tasks before your holiday. All pre-booked activities will be added to a dedicated holiday to-do list, which syncs with your calendar, allowing you to manage your time effectively and enjoy your trip stress-free. Additional features will include viewing and booking cruises, discovering optional events like the Olympics or local free activities, and uploading and sharing photos and reviews of your trip. This project is still in concept phase, and development will begin once the necessary groundwork is completed.
